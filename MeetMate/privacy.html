Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

Overview

MeetMate is a scheduling tool built by GlideNova. This policy explains what information the app stores, what information is sent to third parties only when you use AI features, and what GlideNova does not collect.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your poll data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using SwiftData. This includes poll titles, durations, participant names you type, candidate time slots, vote counts, locked results, and any AI-crafted invite text attached to a poll. GlideNova does not receive a copy of this local data.

Data Sent to Third-Party APIs

MeetMate includes an optional Premium feature called AI Smart Suggestions. When you explicitly type a scheduling prompt and tap the AI button, the text you entered is sent to Gemini through a proxy service so the app can generate suggested time slots and a draft share message. Nothing is sent unless you choose to use that feature.

GlideNova does not use the AI feature to collect background information about you, your device, your contacts, or your location. Only the prompt you intentionally submit for AI processing is transmitted for that request.

Subscriptions and Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. GlideNova does not see or store your credit card number, billing address, or other payment details. If you need billing help or a refund, Apple handles that directly.

No Tracking or Analytics

MeetMate does not use ad tracking, analytics SDKs, or behavioral profiling tools. There are no ads in the app, and GlideNova does not track you across other apps or websites.

Data Retention

Because MeetMate stores your polls locally, your data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the app. GlideNova does not maintain a user account system or a server-side poll history for this app.

Children

MeetMate is not directed to children under 13, and GlideNova does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the effective date above will be updated. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to support@glidenova.app.
