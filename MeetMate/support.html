Frequently asked questions

Is the core feature always free?

Yes. You can create and manage up to 2 polls for free. Premium is only required if you want unlimited polls or AI Smart Suggestions.

What does AI Smart Suggestions do?

AI Smart Suggestions turns a natural-language prompt like “next week after 6, avoid Friday” into recommended time slots. It can also generate a polished invite message you can share after the poll is created.

How do I share a poll?

Open a poll and tap the share button. If the poll was created with AI Smart Suggestions, MeetMate can share the AI-crafted message. Otherwise it shares a plain results summary.

How do I restore Premium on a new device?

Open MeetMate, go to Settings, and tap “Restore Purchases.” As long as you are signed in with the same Apple ID that made the purchase, Apple will restore your subscription status.

How do I cancel?

Go to iOS Settings → tap your Apple ID → Subscriptions → MeetMate → Cancel Subscription. MeetMate does not cancel subscriptions directly because Apple manages billing.

How do refunds work?

All refunds are handled by Apple. If you need one, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with the Apple account used for the purchase.

Still need help?

Email support@glidenova.app and include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.
