Privacy Policy

Effective: March 26, 2026

CalmAnchor is made by GlideNova. We built this app to help people manage anxiety and panic attacks. This policy explains what happens to your information when you use the app.

The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

What stays on your device

Everything you do in CalmAnchor is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Your panic attack journal entries (intensity ratings, trigger notes, resolution notes)
  • Your SOS session history and streak count
  • Your app preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up through iCloud (which is controlled by your Apple ID settings, not by us).

AI features (Gemini)

CalmAnchor's AI features — journal insights and the breathing coach — use Google's Gemini API. When you tap "Analyse" on a journal entry or use the AI breathing coach, the text you have written is sent to Google's servers to generate a response.

Specifically:

  • The text from your journal entry (what you typed in the trigger and resolution fields) is sent to Gemini to generate personalised insights.
  • Your self-reported anxiety level (a number from 1 to 10) is sent to Gemini to select a breathing technique.
  • No other information about you is sent.
  • AI features are only triggered when you explicitly tap the "Analyse" or "Suggest" button — nothing is sent automatically.

Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We recommend not including sensitive personal details in your journal entries if you are concerned about third-party AI processing.

Subscriptions

CalmAnchor Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. We do not use cookies. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Children

CalmAnchor is rated 4+ on the App Store. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else, as we do not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If we ever make changes that affect how your data is handled, we will update this page and change the effective date. We will not retroactively change how data you've already submitted is used.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at support@glidenova.app.
