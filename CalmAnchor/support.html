Frequently asked questions

Is the SOS button always free?

Yes. The core SOS panic attack flow is free, unlimited, and always available — even without a Premium subscription. We will never lock the crisis tool behind a paywall.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone's Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → CalmAnchor → Cancel Subscription. You keep Premium access until the end of the current billing period. We cannot cancel subscriptions on your behalf — this must be done through Apple.

I paid but the app still shows free features. What do I do?

Go to Settings (bottom tab in the app) → tap "Restore Purchases". This re-checks your entitlement with Apple and should unlock Premium immediately. If it doesn't, force-close the app and try again. If the problem persists, email us.

The sounds aren't playing. What's wrong?

Make sure your iPhone is not on Silent mode (the physical switch on the side). Also check that CalmAnchor has not been muted in Settings → Sounds & Haptics. The sounds require the audio files to be bundled with the app — if you just installed a fresh version, try restarting the app.

The AI features say "Check your API key". What does this mean?

AI features require an active internet connection. If you're on a weak connection, try switching to Wi-Fi. If the problem continues, the AI service may be temporarily unavailable — all other app features work without it.

Is my journal data backed up?

Journal entries are stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud backup enabled for your iPhone, your data will be included in your iCloud backup and will restore if you reinstall the app on the same Apple ID. We do not have access to your journal entries.

Can I use the app on my iPad?

Yes. CalmAnchor supports both iPhone and iPad.

I'd like to request a refund.

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, find CalmAnchor in your purchase history, and submit a refund request. We cannot process refunds directly.

Still need help?

Email us at support@glidenova.app and we'll get back to you as soon as we can. Please include your iOS version and a description of the issue.
