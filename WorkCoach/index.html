Features

The motivation layer your focus app has always been missing.

🎤

Audio-Guided Sessions

Select your task, pick a session length (15, 25, 45, or 60 minutes), choose your coach persona, and press start. Your coach delivers motivational prompts at timed intervals — acknowledging milestones, calling out failure points, and building toward the finish line.

🧠

AI-Personalized Scripts

Powered by Gemini, every coaching script is generated fresh based on your exact task, chosen persona, and session length. Instead of generic affirmations, your coach references your actual work: "You're writing that report — halfway there, the hard part is over."

🌟

Three Coach Personas

Intense (direct, pushes you hard), Calm (warm, keeps you grounded), or Encouraging (upbeat, celebrates wins). Choose the voice that resonates with you, or switch it up per session depending on the work.

📈

Session Stats and Streaks

Track completed sessions, total focus hours, and your current streak. Build momentum through visible progress. The numbers don't lie — and neither does your coach.

💬

AI Session Debrief

After each session, your coach generates a short insight on your performance and suggests a follow-up session type based on your patterns. Close every session with a clear picture of where you are.

🎵

Background Audio

Coach audio plays over your background music — Spotify, Brain.fm, whatever you already use. WorkCoach ducks over your music rather than stopping it, so your focus environment stays intact.
