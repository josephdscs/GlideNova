Frequently asked questions

Is the first session always free?

Yes. Your first coaching session is completely free — no subscription required. After that, a 7-day free trial gives you full access to all premium features. The trial converts to a paid subscription ($6.99/month or $39.99/year) unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the trial ends.

What does the AI coach actually do?

Before your session starts, WorkCoach sends your task name, chosen coach persona, and session length to Gemini (Google's AI) to generate a personalized coaching script. The script is broken into timed segments that play at intervals throughout your session — acknowledging where you are in the session, calling out the moments when distraction typically hits, and building toward a finish-line push. Every session script is unique to your task.

How do I change my coach persona?

You can change your coach persona from the Session Setup screen (the home screen) before starting any session — just tap a different persona card. You can also set a default persona in the Settings screen. The three options are Intense (direct, high-energy), Calm (warm, grounding), and Encouraging (upbeat, celebratory).

My coaching audio is not playing. What do I do?

Try the following steps in order: (1) Check your device volume — make sure it is turned up and not muted. (2) Check the Silent Mode switch on the side of your iPhone — flip it to the non-muted position. (3) In WorkCoach Settings, check the Background Audio toggle — make sure it is enabled. (4) If the issue persists, force-quit WorkCoach and reopen it, then start a new session. Audio generation happens at session start, so if there was a network hiccup, a fresh session will retry.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open WorkCoach, navigate to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, so as long as you are signed in with the same account, your purchase will be restored automatically. If you have trouble, make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iOS Settings → tap your name at the top (Apple ID) → Subscriptions → find WorkCoach and tap it → tap "Cancel Subscription." You will retain access until the end of the current billing period. WorkCoach does not handle cancellations directly — all subscription management goes through Apple.
