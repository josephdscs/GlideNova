Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026 — GlideNova

What data ThreeTasks collects

ThreeTasks stores your daily tasks and streak history locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the AI Prioritiser feature (see below).

We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or any form of usage tracking.

AI Prioritiser (Premium feature)

When you use the AI Prioritiser, the text you type into the brain-dump field is sent to a Cloudflare Worker proxy operated by GlideNova, which forwards it to Google Gemini to generate task suggestions. The text is used solely to produce your response and is not stored by GlideNova or retained by the proxy after the request completes.

Google's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

If you do not use the AI Prioritiser, no data leaves your device.

In-app purchases

ThreeTasks Premium is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Apple. GlideNova never receives, stores, or processes your payment information. Apple's Privacy Policy applies to all transactions.

No account required

ThreeTasks does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. The app works entirely offline except for the optional AI feature.

Notifications

If you enable the morning reminder, the notification is scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Children

ThreeTasks is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
