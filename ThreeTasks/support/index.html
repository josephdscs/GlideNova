Is the free version actually useful?

Yes. The core feature — picking 3 tasks per day and tracking your streak — is free, with no time limit and no nag screens. You get 3 daily tasks and 7-day streak history forever at no cost. Premium adds AI suggestions, a home screen widget, and your full streak heatmap.

What does Premium include?

Premium is a one-time purchase of $4.99 (no subscription). It unlocks three features: the AI Prioritiser (paste your brain dump, AI picks your 3 with reasoning), the home screen widget (see and complete tasks without opening the app), and the full streak heatmap (GitHub-style activity calendar for the past year).

How does the AI Prioritiser work?

Tap "AI Pick for Me" on the home screen, paste anything — your email backlog, a Notion dump, a voice note transcript. The AI reads the context, weighs urgency and any deadlines mentioned, and suggests your top 3 with a one-sentence reason for each. You confirm before the tasks are applied. The AI feature requires an internet connection.

How do I add the home screen widget?

Long-press your iPhone home screen until icons jiggle, tap the + button in the top-left corner, search for "ThreeTasks", then choose the small or medium widget. The medium widget shows all 3 tasks with interactive checkboxes — tap a task to mark it complete right from your home screen.

I purchased Premium but it's not showing.

Open ThreeTasks → Settings → tap "Restore Purchase". This re-validates your purchase with Apple. If the issue persists, make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID used for the original purchase, then try again.

How do I get a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, find ThreeTasks in your purchase history, and submit a refund request. Apple typically processes these within 48 hours.
