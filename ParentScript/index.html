Features

Built for the parent who already knows better — and needs the words.

🆘

Tantrum Toolkit — Always Free

A 6-step real-time SOS guide for active meltdowns. Follow the steps while it's happening: regulate yourself first, get close, offer connection, move through it together. Never paywalled, ever.

📚

Script Library

Browse by situation — hitting, not listening, public meltdown, bedtime refusal, sharing, whining, and more. Filter by your child's age. Each card shows what to say and what not to say, with a brief explanation of why.

✨

Ask AI

Describe your specific situation in plain words. ParentScript's AI returns why this behavior is normal at your child's age, three exact scripts tailored to the moment, phrases that tend to backfire, and one preventive tip.

🃏

Daily Script Card

A new "Say This, Not That" card every day, drawn from real situations. The format your brain recognizes instantly — wrong phrase, why it backfires, replacement scripts.

🔖

Saved Favorites

Save the scripts that work for your child. Build a personal reference library of what actually resonates. Access your favorites instantly — no searching under pressure.

🎯

Age-Filtered Content

Scripts calibrated for toddlers ages 1–6. What works for a 2-year-old is different from what works for a 5-year-old. Filter by your child's age and get language they can actually process.
