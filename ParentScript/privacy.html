Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 13, 2026

The short version

ParentScript does not require an account. We do not collect your personal information. Your data lives on your device. The only data that ever leaves your device is text you explicitly type into the Ask AI feature — and that goes to Google's AI service to generate a response.

What we collect

Nothing automatically. ParentScript does not collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or any personal information. No account is required to use the app.

Your child's information

If you set your child's age during onboarding, that preference is stored locally on your device using SwiftData — Apple's on-device data framework. It never leaves your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

Ask AI feature

When you use the Ask AI feature, the text you type is sent to Google's Gemini AI service to generate parenting scripts. This text is processed through a Cloudflare Worker proxy — GlideNova does not log, store, or retain your prompts or the AI responses. Google's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Do not include sensitive personal information in your AI prompts.

Subscriptions and payments

All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. GlideNova never sees your payment card details or billing information. Subscription management is handled entirely by Apple. For billing questions, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

Local storage

ParentScript stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your child's age preference (from onboarding)
  • Scripts you save to Favorites
  • Your subscription status (verified with Apple)
  • Your library lookup count (used to determine free tier limits)

This data is not synced to any server. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

No tracking or advertising

ParentScript does not contain third-party advertising SDKs, cross-app tracking, or behavioral analytics. We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with any third party except as described in the Ask AI section above.

Children's privacy

ParentScript is designed for parents, not children. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for adults using it to support their own parenting.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email us at support@glidenova.app.
