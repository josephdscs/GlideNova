Frequently asked questions

Is the Tantrum Toolkit always free?

Yes — permanently and unconditionally. The Tantrum Toolkit (the 6-step real-time SOS guide) is always free, requires no account, and will never be paywalled. We believe a crisis tool should never be gated. Tap "I need help right now" on the home screen at any time, subscription or not.

What's included in the free tier?

The free tier includes: the Tantrum Toolkit (unlimited, always free), the Script Library for ages 1–4 (up to 3 situation lookups per session), and a daily tip card. Premium unlocks unlimited lookups, ages 5–6 scripts, unlimited Ask AI, and the ability to save favorites.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone's Settings app, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find ParentScript and tap Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep premium access until then. You can also cancel via the App Store app under your account profile.

Ask AI isn't working — what should I check?

First, make sure you have an active internet connection — Ask AI requires a network request to generate responses. If the connection is fine, try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, the AI service may be temporarily unavailable. Wait a few minutes and try again. If Ask AI is still not working after an hour, contact us at support@glidenova.app with a description of what happens when you try to send a prompt.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, not your device. Download ParentScript on your new device, sign in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe, and your premium access will be restored automatically when the app checks your subscription status. If it doesn't restore, open the app and look for a "Restore Purchases" option in Settings. Make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID.

I was charged but the app says I'm on the free tier. What do I do?

This sometimes happens when there's a sync delay between Apple's servers and the app. Close the app completely, wait 30 seconds, and reopen it — the subscription status should refresh. If you're still showing as a free user after reopening, go to Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. Still having trouble? Email us at support@glidenova.app with your Apple ID (email only — not your password) and we'll help sort it out.

Where does the parenting guidance in this app come from?

The scripts and guidance in ParentScript are grounded in established positive parenting and Montessori principles — approaches developed and refined by child development researchers and educators over decades. They are not proprietary to any single source.
